# 🟢 LangChain vs. LangGraph vs. Deep Agents

<mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Deep Agents:**</mark>

* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">Automatic context compression, a virtual filesystem, and subagent-spawning</mark>



<mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**LangChain:**</mark>

* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">Highly customizable harness, easily tailored to your use case and data</mark>



<mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**LangGraph:**</mark>

* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">Low-level orchestration framework, for advanced needs combining deterministic and agentic workflows</mark>



<mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**LangSmith:**</mark>

* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">To trace, debug, and evaluate agents</mark>
* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">Set</mark> <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;"></mark><mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">`LANGSMITH_TRACING=true`</mark> <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;"></mark><mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">and your API key to get started</mark>

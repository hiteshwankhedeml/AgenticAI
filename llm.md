# LLM

* Next word predictor
* A large language model has read an enormous slice of everything humans have ever written, and learned — purely from patterns — what word is statistically most likely to come next, given everything written so far.&#x20;
* There's no separate "understanding module" hidden inside anywhere. The feeling of being understood emerges entirely from being extremely good at this one narrow job, repeated one word at a time until a full answer exists.
